LOCALIZE

city review app

Why Localize?

When you're taking a trip to a new place, how do you figure out what restaurants to go to? Where to see the coolest local bands play? The best bars hang out at, galleries to visit, and parks to stroll through?

Most people use Yelp, but many of us wish we had a friend in the city to give us their personal, curated recommendations. Localize brings you a city’s inside scoop by the people who know it best, its locals.

Using Localize

This prototype focuses on recommendations for Portland, Maine with the idea that it could expand to other cities. If it’s your first time visiting the app, click on the help link in the navigation bar, or simply click on the get started button to enter the main maps page. If you’d like to experience the app from a local’s persepctive, click on the sign up link.

</> React/Redux, Node.js/Express, Passport, PostgreSQL
